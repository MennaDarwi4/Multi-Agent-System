Analyst note (internal), July 2026.

DevOps and SRE hiring across Egypt and the wider MENA region accelerated sharply
in the first half of 2026, with especially strong demand for Jenkins, Kubernetes,
and Terraform skills. Several regional banks began migrating core workloads to
managed cloud, and backup / disaster-recovery spend is now a board-level priority.

On the competitive side: Acme is winning mid-market deals primarily on price after
cutting cloud list prices ~15%. Globex is pushing upmarket with a premium AI add-on
tier aimed at enterprises. Initech is expanding into the Gulf with a partner-led
model. Customer churn is trending down industry-wide as retention programs mature,
while cloud revenue share keeps climbing quarter over quarter.
